1		Tokenize			Split the line into tokens (usually by spaces and operators), respecting quotes.
2		Quote Removal		Strip quote characters (' or ") but remember their context.
3		Variable Expansion	Expand $VAR, $?, etc. But NOT inside '...'.
4		Word Splitting		Unquoted strings containing spaces (like $HOME) may split into multiple argv.
5		Globbing			Expand wildcards like *.c (only for bonus).
